Account Management

Delete Your Account

Weather Tether  ·  Permanent account and data removal

This action is permanent. Deleting your account will immediately and irreversibly remove all your data from our servers. This cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your email address and authentication record (Firebase Authentication)
  • All saved city locations — City 1, City 2, and City 3 (Firebase Firestore)
  • Your local session file on the device

Deletion is immediate and cannot be reversed. You would need to create a new account to use Weather Tether again.

1

Delete From Within the App Recommended

The fastest way to delete your account is directly from the app:

  1. Open Weather Tether on your Android device
  2. Tap the side panel and go to Settings
  3. Scroll down to Delete Account
  4. Enter your email and password to confirm
  5. Tap Delete Account — your account and all data will be removed immediately
2

Request Deletion via GitHub If you can't access the app

If you no longer have access to the app or your device, you can request manual deletion by opening an issue on our GitHub repository. Please include the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 days.
